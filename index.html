NISHAN INDUSTRIES LIMITED was incorporated in 1987, located at No.25 Reclamation Road Port Harcourt, Rivers State. A 100% Nigerian company with the desire to contribute meaningfully to Nigerian's growth, installed and operate world class industrial mineral processing Plant and provide ancillary support to satisfy local demand and export surplus production.

Our Vision

To be the Company of Choice for the supply of Drilling support equipment and materials to the Nation's Oil and Gas industry.

To achieve engineering excellence, on every project, without compromising quality, standard and competitive pricing.

To promote and encourage the sourcing and utilization of local raw materials to achieve industry self-sufficiency.

Our Mission

Applying environmental, health and safety best practices in achieving operational excellence with personnel welfare and operating environment at the focal point;

To generate a return on investment that meets the shareholders' criteria;

To be a responsible corporate citizen of the country.
